Troubleshooting

Setup is taking a long time
Normal on a slow connection ‚Äî can take up to 10 minutes. Leave Terminal open until you see ‚úÖ Setup complete!
"Cannot open Open Medical Booklet.command" security warning
macOS sometimes blocks files downloaded from the internet. Right-click Open Medical Booklet.command and choose Open ‚Äî you'll see a prompt asking if you're sure. Click Open. After this once, double-clicking works normally.
The app opens but shows "config.yaml not found"
Use the double-click method (Finder ‚Üí Documents ‚Üí medical-booklet ‚Üí Open Medical Booklet.command), or the Terminal command from Step 3 above, which includes the full path.
"No module named streamlit" or similar error
Setup didn't finish. Open Terminal and run:

cd ~/Documents/medical-booklet && bash setup.sh
PDF generates but is blank or looks wrong
Open Terminal and run:

cd ~/Documents/medical-booklet && bash setup.sh

Then close Terminal completely and try launching again.
"Port 8501 already in use"
The app is already running. Go to http://localhost:8501 in your browser ‚Äî or find the Terminal window and press Ctrl+C to stop it first.
Setup asks for a password and I'm not sure what to enter
Your normal Mac login password ‚Äî the same one you type to unlock your screen. Not an IT or admin credential. If you don't know it, contact your IT helpdesk.
